# AI Commerce Glossary

A public glossary of concepts used in AI Commerce Intelligence.

## Core Terms

### AI Readability
The ability of AI systems to access and parse content.

### AI Understanding
The ability of AI systems to correctly interpret products, categories, and brand information.

### AI Trust
The level of confidence AI systems have in a brand or product.

### Recommendation Visibility
The frequency with which a brand appears in AI-generated recommendations.

### Recommendation Confidence
The confidence score associated with a recommendation.

### Recommendation Share
The percentage of recommendations captured by a brand within a category.

### Machine Readable Commerce
Commerce infrastructure optimized for AI interpretation and recommendation.

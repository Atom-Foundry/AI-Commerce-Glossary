## Definition

Recommendation Intelligence‚ĄĘ is the discipline of measuring, analyzing, and understanding how AI systems recommend brands, products, services, and businesses.

It focuses on recommendation behavior rather than visibility alone.

While traditional search analytics measures rankings, traffic, and impressions, Recommendation Intelligence measures which entities AI systems choose to recommend, how often they are recommended, how prominently they appear, and how recommendation behavior changes over time.

The field is based on a simple observation:

Visibility is not recommendation.

A business can be visible to AI systems without being recommended. Likewise, a business can be recommended despite having limited visibility.

Recommendation Intelligence seeks to explain this gap.

Core areas of study include recommendation frequency, recommendation share, recommendation position, recommendation confidence, recommendation consistency, and recommendation influence across AI systems.

As commerce increasingly shifts from search-driven discovery to AI-assisted decision-making, Recommendation Intelligence provides a framework for understanding how recommendations shape consideration, selection, and purchasing behavior.

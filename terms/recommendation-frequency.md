## Definition

Recommendation Frequency‚ĄĘ measures how often a brand, product, business, or entity appears across a defined set of AI recommendation opportunities.

It represents the consistency of recommendation behavior.

Recommendation Frequency‚ĄĘ is calculated as:

**Recommendation Frequency = Recommendation Appearances √∑ Total Recommendation Opportunities**

If a brand appears in 200 out of 400 AI recommendation runs, its Recommendation Frequency‚ĄĘ is 50%.

Unlike Recommendation Share‚ĄĘ, which measures the proportion of all recommendations captured, Recommendation Frequency‚ĄĘ measures how consistently a brand appears across recommendation events.

The metric helps answer a simple question:

**How often does AI choose to include this brand?**

High Recommendation Frequency‚ĄĘ indicates that a brand is repeatedly selected across different prompts, sessions, or recommendation opportunities.

Low Recommendation Frequency‚ĄĘ indicates that a brand appears only occasionally or under specific conditions.

Recommendation Frequency‚ĄĘ is a core metric within Recommendation Intelligence‚ĄĘ and is commonly analyzed alongside:

* Recommendation Share‚ĄĘ
* Recommendation Position‚ĄĘ
* Recommendation Confidence‚ĄĘ

A brand may have high Recommendation Share‚ĄĘ but low Recommendation Frequency‚ĄĘ if it dominates only a small number of recommendation scenarios.

Likewise, a brand may have high Recommendation Frequency‚ĄĘ but lower Recommendation Share‚ĄĘ if it appears consistently but rarely occupies the top recommendation positions.

As AI systems increasingly influence purchasing decisions, Recommendation Frequency‚ĄĘ provides a measure of recommendation consistency and recommendation presence over time.
